About the project

Garden Hills Residence is a promising new project located on the border of Prague's Vršovice and Královské Vinohrady. It is therefore one of the most attractive districts in Prague.
This residence consists of sixteen modern apartment units, fifteen of which are 2+kk and on the top floor of this new residence we can find a spacious 4+kk apartment with a balcony. Some apartments have a front garden or balcony.

Modern interior design, developed by Italian architects and complemented by quality workmanship, creates an ideal environment for comfortable long-term living or investment. The apartment is designed in classic standard including floor coverings, tiles, white paint, wooden windows and doors. The choice of standards was carefully made in collaboration with architects and leading contractors to meet exacting design and utility requirements.

There will be 13 garage spaces and 13 basements.

The apartment building is located in an attractive location in the centre of Prague 10 - Vršovice in Košická Street. This location is important for its accessibility and a wide range of services and shops in the vicinity. Near the house there are tram and bus stops Ruská and Krymská. In the vicinity you will find all services, including kindergartens, schools, pharmacies, banks, post offices, shops and various businesses. Next to the house there are Havlíčkův sady - Grébovka and within walking distance also Sámův park and Heroldovy sady.

Estimated project completion: Summer 2025.
